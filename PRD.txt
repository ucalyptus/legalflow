## **1. Overview**

**Product Name**: LegalFlow
**Version**: V1
**Objective**: LegalFlow is a legal workflow automation and management platform designed for Indian law firms and legal practitioners. The MVP aims to streamline case management, document handling, filings, and AI-powered legal assistance.

## **2. Information Architecture (IA)**

**Top Bar:**

- Title/Path
- Search Bar

**Right Tab:**

- Notifications
- Activity Log

**Left Tab (Navigation Panel):**

- Dashboard
- Cases
- Documents
- Ask AI
- Filings
- Settings
- Account

## **3. Feature Specifications**

### **3.1 Dashboard**

Widgets:

- **Action Items Widget**: Displays tasks that require immediate attention (e.g., pending reviews, due filings, case updates).
- **Cases Overview**: Summarizes active cases, status, deadlines, and important milestones.
- **Filings & Deadlines**: Highlights upcoming legal filings and deadlines.
- **Key Performance Metrics**:
    - Number of active cases
    - Pending documents for review
    - Filings submitted this month
    - AI-assisted legal research completed
- **Calendar View**: Integrates court dates, internal deadlines, and filings.
- **Suggested Additions**:
    - Recently accessed cases/documents
    - Customizable KPI widgets based on user roles

### **3.2 Cases Management**

- **Active Case Widget**: Highlights ongoing critical cases.
- **Cases List View**:
    - Case Title
    - Client Name
    - People Involved
    - Case Type
    - Status (Ongoing, Settled, Under Review, etc.)
    - Milestone/Deadline
    - Actions (Edit, Assign, Update Status, etc.)
    - Notifications (New filings, hearing dates, critical updates)
    - **Action CTA**: Quick actions such as updating case status, adding notes, or sharing with team members.
- **Functionalities**:
    - Sorting and Filtering (by date, status, priority, client, etc.)
    - CRUD Operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete cases)
    - **Backward Compatibility**:
        - **Automated Data Extraction**: Use AI-powered document parsing tools to extract structured data from case files.
        - **Bulk Import Tools**: Allow law firms to upload existing case records in CSV, JSON, or XML format for migration.
        - **API Integrations**: Connect with existing case management systems to pull structured case metadata.
        - **Field Mapping Engine**: Create a system that maps existing case attributes to LegalFlowâ€™s data model.
        - **Custom Mapping Rules**: Provide flexibility for firms to define their own mapping if their old system has a unique structure.
        - **Document Versioning**: Maintain old case files and attach them to new cases while retaining historical versions.
        - **AI-Powered Categorization**: Use AI to classify and tag old case documents, filings, and client data for easy retrieval.
        - **Search & Indexing**: Enable full-text search across old and new case files to make past case law and filings easily accessible.
        - **Parallel Processing Mode**: Allow firms to continue using their old system while LegalFlow runs alongside, gradually integrating old cases.
        - **Data Validation & Audit Logs**: Ensure all migrated cases comply with data integrity and legal regulations.

### **3.3 Documents Management**

- **Documents List View**:
    - Document Title
    - Associated Case Title
    - Status (Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved)
    - Document Type (Contract, Pleading, Agreement, etc.)
    - **Actions CTA** (Edit, Download, Share, File Online)
- **Functionalities**:
    - Sorting and Filtering (by status, type, date, case association, etc.)
    - CRUD Operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete documents)
    - AI-powered document search and categorization
    - Bulk actions (approve, delete, archive, share)
    - **Automatic Drafting**: If a previously drafted document is uploaded, the system suggests a structured draft based on similar past documents.
    - **Drag & Drop Clause Library**: Users can select and insert predefined clauses, paragraphs, and templates into drafts.

### **3.3.1 Expanded Document View**

- **Document Display**: Rich text or Markdown format
- **Editing & Annotation**: Acrobat Pro/Word Doc-like functionalities
- **AI-powered Operations**:
    - **Draft**: Generate legal drafts based on past cases/precedents
    - **Summarize**: Provide concise overviews of documents
    - **Review**: AI-assisted review of legal documents
    - **Suggest**: AI-driven modifications and improvements
    - **Extract**: Extract key dates, events, clauses

### **3.4 Ask AI (Legal AI Assistant)**

- **Chat Interface**: Open-ended conversational AI for legal queries
- **Capabilities**:
    - Access legal precedents and laws
    - Retrieve case-specific and document-based insights
    - Summarize complex legal texts
    - Provide AI-powered legal strategy suggestions
    - Assist in document drafting and contract review

### **3.5 Filings Management**

- **Objective**: Provide a seamless interface to file legal documents with government platforms (e-Courts, Tribunals, etc.).
- **Key Features**:
    - Pre-filled form templates based on case data
    - Integration with government e-filing portals
    - Real-time status tracking of filings
    - Notifications for deadlines and submission updates

### **3.6 Email Ingestion & Case Data Forwarding**

- **Objective**: Enable users to forward emails to a dedicated workspace email address that automatically processes and links information to relevant cases.
- **Key Features**:
    - Each case is assigned a unique workspace email ID.
    - Incoming emails are parsed to extract attachments, metadata, and relevant content.
    - Automatic categorization and association of forwarded emails with the appropriate case.
    - AI-powered extraction of key legal information from email content.
    - Notifications for new email data linked to a case.
    - Search and filter capabilities for all forwarded emails within a case.

## **4. Design & User Experience**

- **Minimalistic UI with a focus on efficiency**
- **Dark & Light Mode Support**
- **Drag-and-drop file uploads in Documents and Filings**
- **Role-based access for different users (Lawyers, Paralegals, Clients)**
- **Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access**
